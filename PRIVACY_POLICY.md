# Privacy Policy — Vasuki

Effective date: 2026-02-16

Vasuki ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

## 1) Data We Collect
Vasuki may process the following information provided by you inside the app:
- Birth details entered by you (name, date/time of birth, place of birth, latitude/longitude if available)
- Chart preferences and analysis inputs
- Optional life-event notes entered by you

## 2) How Data Is Used
We use this data only to:
- Generate kundali charts and astrology calculations
- Show analysis screens (e.g., strengths, predictions, remedies)
- Improve app stability and user experience

## 3) Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data.
We do not share personal data with advertisers.
Data may be processed by platform services required to run the app (for example, app store distribution or crash/runtime infrastructure).

## 4) Data Storage
User-entered data is primarily stored on-device/local app storage unless a future feature explicitly enables cloud sync.

## 5) Permissions
The app may request limited device permissions only when required for functionality.
We avoid unnecessary sensitive permissions.

## 6) Children’s Privacy
Vasuki is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will review and remove it where applicable.

## 7) Your Choices
You may:
- Edit or remove data entered in the app
- Uninstall the app to remove local app data from your device

## 8) Security
We take reasonable technical measures to protect data processed by the app. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## 9) Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at the same URL with a revised effective date.
